    What's our Mission and Vision?

    Vision

    We envision a dynamic and inclusive digital learning environment where students can effectively grasp the core principles of 2D animation through interactive and engaging educational resources

    Mission

    Our mission is to offer college students an interactive, engaging web-based platform to enhance their understanding of 2D digital animation principles, incorporating tutorials, multimedia presentations, and hands-on exercises.

    What is University of Rizal System's Goal?

    National

    To train the nation’s manpower in the skills required for the national development for the improvement of the quality of human life

    Regional

    To train the nation’s manpower in the development of skills, attitude and values required for regional development. University : To develop the full potential of an individual in academic and technological discipline for an empowered, productive and morally upright citizenry.
